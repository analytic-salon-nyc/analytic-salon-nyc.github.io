About

This is a drop-in salon for intelligent discussion. If you ever heard about analytic philosophy but did not know where to start or wanted to learn a bit more, this might be for you. Do you have an appetite for puzzles, sharp questions, and mindf*? Do you value clean thinking, rigorous argumentation, but friendly and respectful discussion? Do you think logic should play a fundamental role in how we debate? Then this is definitely for you. Each session is a guided, respctful discussion. The best part? We will bring real philosophers to be among us and help us out. No jargon, no prior philosophy background, and no homework required.

This isn’t an unstructured meetup, but it also isn’t a lecture. You’ll meet philosophers and advanced researchers who will help us explore the often elusive world of analytic philosophy. Along the way, you may inadvertently sharpen an argument, clarify a concept, draw a subtle distinction, and think like a philosopher.

Seats are very limited. RSVP required. 100% Free.

Format

Each meeting is 90 minutes and self-contained.

  • 0–10 arrivals + a light opening prompt
  • 10–20 the puzzle, presented crisply (no theory yet)
  • 20–65 structured small-group conversation (guided prompts)
  • 65–85 full-room shareback + best arguments
  • 85–90 one explicit takeaway move + teaser for next time

Optional: a nearby post-salon hang for anyone who wants to keep talking. Optional: a short reading/video sent after RSVP (only if you want).
